Reflections of a Casual Bouldering Enthusiast

Chalk dust and bad decisions

A defense of trying a move again after it has already humiliated you.

Mar 27, 2026

The ordinary comedy of bouldering is that you keep returning to the same small failure as though repetition might embarrass it into becoming success.

There is a quiet dignity in falling off something on purpose, provided you do it with enough commitment to look intentional.
